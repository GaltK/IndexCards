# Product Overview

IndexCards is a cloud-native card collection platform that enables users to manage their personal trading card collections. Users can catalog cards with detailed attributes (player names, brand, series, type, rarity), organize them into custom collections, and showcase their top 20 favorite cards on their home page.

## Key Features

- Card library management with full CRUD operations
- Advanced filtering, sorting, and pagination for large collections
- Custom collections for organizing cards by user-defined criteria
- "Top Cards" showcase (max 20 cards) automatically created for each user
- User profiles with personal information and bio
- Secure authentication via Amazon Cognito with OpenID Connect support

## Target Users

Collectors who want to digitally catalog and organize their physical trading card collections with a modern, accessible web interface.
